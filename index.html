About Me

Crafting Spaces
with Intent & Soul

With years of experience in architecture and interior design, Sugi M has crafted spaces that blend aesthetics with functionality — from luxury residences and corporate offices to hospitality projects and vibrant public spaces across Chennai and beyond.

As the founder of Plan Spacez, the philosophy is simple: every space tells a story. The design approach marries bold architectural vision with the warmth of thoughtful interiors — creating environments that inspire, comfort, and endure.
